Hello, I am Tersiteab Adem! I am a second year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Michigan. Currently, I am advised by Professor Valeria Bertacco. My interest lies within the domain of architectural support for energy-constrained embedded systems and novel devices like Quantum Computers. I graduated with BSc in Electrical and Computer Engineering, Computer Engineering major from Addis Ababa University in 2020. In my free time, I enjoy reading books, board games and stargazing.
